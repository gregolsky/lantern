================================================================================
  LANTERN — IMAGE GENERATION PROMPTS
  Style guide + placements for all graphics in the app
================================================================================

CONSISTENT STYLE (paste this into every prompt)
------------------------------------------------
Cozy fantasy oil painting illustration in the style of the Golden Frog Inn
Instagram account — warm candlelit tavern atmosphere, soft loose brushwork,
painterly impasto texture, aged canvas grain, rich amber and deep indigo
tones, whimsical and inviting, reminiscent of a hand-painted inn sign or
storybook cover. NOT a cartoon, NOT flat vector art — painted, warm,
dimensional. Square 1:1 format. NO text or letters in the image.
STRICT COLOUR PALETTE — matches the Lantern app's color scheme, washed out
and desaturated as if printed on aged paper. Use ONLY these tones, no others:
  • Near-black slate  #0D1117  — backgrounds, deep shadow (app: gray-950)
  • Dark stone        #1C2333  — walls, secondary surfaces (app: gray-900)
  • Warm parchment    #C8C0A8  — light areas, faces, paper (app: gray-100 muted)
  • Faded indigo      #6060A0  — primary accent, UI glow (app: indigo-600 washed)
  • Dusty sky blue    #3A6A88  — ISO 27001 elements (app: sky-700 faded)
  • Muted amber       #A07030  — NIS2 / fire / lantern glow (app: amber-600 faded)
  • Brick rose        #883A48  — HIPAA / danger / warning (app: rose-600 faded)
  • Sage teal         #2A7A58  — GDPR / nature / growth (app: emerald-600 faded)
All colors desaturated ~35% and darkened slightly vs the app originals, as if
viewed through a warm dusty filter or printed on aged newsprint. No vivid
neon, no pure white, no fully saturated hues anywhere.

MASTER CHARACTER DESCRIPTION (include in every prompt that features him)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The hero: a whimsical elderly wizard with a very long flowing white beard,
a tall crooked pointy hat in faded deep indigo with a slight slouch, and a
long flowing robe in dusty indigo-purple with amber trim and worn patches.
He carries a gnarled wooden staff topped with a softly glowing amber crystal
lantern. Round wire spectacles perched on his nose. Warm kind expression,
slightly bumbling but undeniably wise. Rosy cheeks, expressive bushy white
eyebrows. Painted in a loose warm Golden Frog Inn oil-painting style —
soft brushwork, rich candlelit depth, aged canvas texture. Colors from the
washed-out palette: faded indigo robes, amber lantern-glow, warm parchment
beard and skin, brick rose accent details, near-black slate shadows.
This same wizard appears in EVERY image — he is the consistent protagonist
of the Lantern compliance adventure.
